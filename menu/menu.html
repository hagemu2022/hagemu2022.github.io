Hagemu Sushi and Ramen Bar Menu

Hagemu Sushi and Ramen Bar offers high class and authentic Japanese cuisine only prepared with the freshest and finest ingredients. We at Hagemu Sushi and Ramen Bar are honored to serve you with excellence and gratitude.

We envision to enlarge our regional footprint, to be the most preferred and to be the house hold name of Japanese cuisine. Our goal is to expand further to let everyone experience a unique approach to Japanese cuisine.

Douzo meshiagare! Enjoy your meals!

Sashimi

Sashimi サシミ is a Japanese delicacy consisting of fresh raw fish or meat sliced into thin pieces. Sashimi is often the first course in a formal Japanese meal.

  • More

Zensai

Zensai ゼンサイ is the “first-served-dish” or an appetizer served at the beginning of meals that shows the talent of the cooks by the way their colors and shapes are elaborated and beautifully dished up.

  • More

Fusion Maki

Fusion Maki is a combination maki made up of cooked or raw seafood and vegetables rolled up inside of seaweed and rice.

  • More

Hoso Maki

Hoso Maki is a traditional maki roll that has only one single ingredient.

  • More

Nigiri

Nigiri ニギリズシ is a slice of fish or other seafood ingredient on a hand-pressed rectangular rice block commonly known as the Japanese sushi.

  • More

Platter

Platter offers a collection of assorted maki, nigiri, and/or sashimi to share with your family and friends.

  • More

Sarada

Sarada サラダ is a salad made with fresh vegetables and seafood loaded with contrasting textures, tastes, and colors. It is dressed with in-house Hagemu sauce or Japanese mayonnaise.

  • More

Gohan

Gohan ゴハン is a collection of different fried rice sautéed with butter, egg, and vegetables.

  • More

Ramen

Ramen ラメン is a Japanese dish with a translation of "pulled noodles" typically served in broth with meat and vegetables.

  • More

Chef's Roll

Undecided what sushi roll you really want? Chef's roll is made from our best available ingredients rolled up to the finest possible maki.

  • More

Donburi

Donburi ドンブリ combines a bowl of steamed rice, meat, vegetables, and sauce for an all-in-one meal that's both convenient and filling.

  • More

Teppanyaki

Teppanyaki テッパンヤキ is griddled thin slices of meat, or fish, seafood, vegetables and noodles quickly fried on a hotplate.

  • More

Teriyaki

Teriyaki テリヤキ is one of the most popular Japanese cooking method in which the meat are broiled or grilled with a glaze of soy sauce, mirin, and sugar.

  • More

Katsu

Katsu カツ丼 is a deep-fried breaded meat cutlet, usually topped with egg, vegetables, and condiments.

  • More

Bento

Bento べんとう is a Japanese-style lunchbox composed of a rice and meat and an assortment of pickled or cooked vegetables.

  • More

Dried Noodles

Choose between udon or soba, Japanese dried noodles stir fried with an assortment of vegetables and meat.
