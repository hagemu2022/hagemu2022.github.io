Terms and Conditions

HAGEMU SUSHI AND RAMEN BAR wants your event to be successful and the best way to achieve this is to formulate a strong, honest and open relationship with you, where communication is clear and expectations from both sides are understood from the outset.

Payment Terms

As many costs are incurred on your behalf prior to the event date, HAGEMU SUSHI AND RAMEN BAR have the following payment terms:

  • A deposit, based on a minimum of 50% of the total quotation costs, is required immediately to confirm your reservation/event.
  • Full payment of all known costs is payable before or on the date of the said event.
HAGEMU SUSHI AND RAMEN BAR is payable by cash or thru card and bank transfer.

Cancelation

By any instance that you need to cancel your event or reservation, the following terms will apply:

  • All cancelation must be made by phone call or personal appearance a week before the event.
  • If client shall cancel or be deemed to cancel the event or reservation 7 days before the event date, then the client shall be liable to pay 20% of total costs.
  • If client shall cancel or be deemed to cancel the event or reservation 3 days before the event date, then the client shall be liable to pay 35% of total costs.
  • If client shall cancel or be deemed to cancel the event or reservation on the day of the event date, then the client shall be liable to pay 100% of total costs.
