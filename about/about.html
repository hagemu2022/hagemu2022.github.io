Brief History

"To make it big in the culinary world through precise and excellent skill." This was an aspiration shared by two men, who, driven with much passion, pursued their culinary dreams overseas in the year 2012. While abroad, they were both hired as cooks under the careful supervision of one of the most respected master chef in Japan, Hagemu Nishimura. Equally as dedicated as the young men he called his students, Hagemu Nimishura skillfully enhanced and bestowed every ounce of his culinary knowledge upon his cooks producing the outstanding chefs they are now. However, after more than six years of detailed hands-on training, Chef Hagemu made the decision of hanging up his apron and took a break.

The two brothers, more experienced than ever, then came up with the idea of putting a restaurant that would serve nothing but authentic Japanese dishes imparted to them by their master. This idea of theirs turned into sweet reality, although they had one more thing left to deal with. They weren't so certain what to call their newly built home. After some time of thinking for the perfect name, they finally came up with the decision to name the restaurant after the great master chef himself; not only to pay him respect but also as a constant reminder of their remarkable and one-of-a-kind friendship.
